MediaWorks Overview & Services

What is MediaWorks used for?

MediaWorks is HCA's online tool for sharing, customizing, and managing digital and print-ready assets. Access images, content, video, print collateral and other media assets for a variety of service lines, initiatives and campaigns. From this enterprise repository, you can distribute collateral by downloading, sending to print, posting to social and sharing.

Who manages the Mediaworks site and its content?

The Creative Services team manages the MediaWorks platform and evaluates assets for brand alignment, quality and consistency. Thanks to the many contributors from across the enterprise, MediaWorks is a robust centralized repository of shared images, content, video, print collateral, and other media assets.

How do I update an existing asset or add a new one to MediaWorks?

Please submit a request through our intake form to add or update an asset on MediaWorks. Please note that not all MediaWorks assets are owned by Creative Services and some requests may need to be approved by a business owner.

What's the best way to find something in MediaWorks?

Try one of these options from the top navigation bar: the search field or Category menu.

Search. If you can’t find what you are looking for on MediaWorks, try using the search bar in the top navigation bar. Use the toggles to the right of the search bar to choose between searching the entire site or within your current category.

Advanced Search.Use advanced search to narrow your search results. This is helpful if you have an idea of date range, division, service line, etc. that you would like to search. To access advanced search options, click the arrow in the search field and click “Advanced Search.”

Category Menu You can explore assets and images by topic using the Category drop-down menu

Or, click the links in the Categories listed on the MediaWorks home page to explore available assets. If you are unable to find something, contact MediaWorks Support.

Managing Your Account

Viewing your saved templates

Each time you customize a template and click the “Save & Continue” button, your template is saved into a library within your MediaWorks account called “My Documents”. You find your “My Documents” by navigating to Manage and clicking “My Documents”.

How to view your order history

To view previous MediaWorks orders, click on “Orders” in the top navigation, and choose “Order History”. This will provide a list of past orders including Order ID, Date, Cost, and Status. You have the ability to search and sort through your orders to quickly find what you are looking for.

Update email preferences

MediaWorks sends out a few automated emails to notify you about product updates and approvals. To subscribe to these emails, go to your profile settings by hovering over your profile icon in the top right corner and choosing “Profile”. At the bottom of the page, you can choose your email preferences. Find more information on the MediaWorks email alerts below.

Content Alerts: MediaWorks sends monthly emails containing a list of key products that have been updated, so you can be sure to have the latest and greatest. You can also subscribe to assets you are interested in to be included in your monthly updated product email. Certain assets on MediaWorks are set to be in monthly automated emails. Some assets on MediaWorks display a “Notify me when updated” button which will subscribe you to an automated monthly email

Approval Alerts: Certain templates on MediaWorks require the business owner to approve your custom piece before it can be downloaded or set to print. This is to ensure you are supplied with a quality product. Approvals are required for TriStar templates.

MediaWorks Orders

Placing a print order through MediaWorks

One benefit of using MediaWorks is that assets can be customized and ordered in just a few clicks. We partner with multiple print centers across the country who have gone through a rigorous process to help ensure quality, cost efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Learn more about our print centers.

How do I find out the status of my order?

Turn around time for orders are typically 5-7 business days or less. For large quantities or slightly complex products, it could take about 10 business days.

How do I check on my order status or change my order?

The MediaWorks team cannot make any changes to your order after it has been placed. If you have a question about your order or need to update order details, please contact the print vendor directly as soon as possible. Be sure to include your MediaWorks order number.
